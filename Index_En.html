Punta Cana Bar

You'll find Dominican Republic in Bordeaux

Punta Cana Bar is a unique place of its kind. We travel thousands of miles just by walking through the door.

The scenery with warm colours, the salsa, merengue or kizomba music, the calm and calm atmosphere in the afternoon, much more dynamic in the evening... Everything could make us believe that we are in the heart of Punta Cana.

But it is in the Stalingrad district that Punta Cana Bar opened its doors 4 years ago to the delight of rum lovers.

Our evenings in Punta Cana bar

Every week, evenings and events around Latin cultures are organized. They are opportunities to have a good time until dawn.

From 4pm to 8pm, it's Happy Hour. Cocktails are €5, XL cocktails €9 and draught beers from €2.10.

Finally, every Wednesday evening a Dominican group comes to play its most beautiful songs and invites you to travel in a Caliente atmosphere.

The Punta Cana bar menu

At Punta Cana you will enjoy the best Dominican cocktails prepared in accordance with tradition. You will also find a wide choice of alcoholic and non-alcoholic, exotic and traditional drinks that you can accompany with tapas.
